Now that you have had the chance to get to know the grid and how the co-ordinate system works, you have a chance to create a password of your own. In this page the same keyboard inputs apply, W A S and D to move you can use M to toggle mute for the grid cell reading, use N to toggle the graphics on screen and R to reset your position in the grid.

Your password will consist of the combination of keys you input.

You must complete 5 movements, which will cover 6 grid squares including your original one.

You may begin in any grid square you chose, this must be entered in the text box below as a letter, representing your starting column, followed by a number, representing the row which you wish to begin. an example might be F6 for the bottom right position or A3 for the third down cell in the leftmost column.

These movements can be in any direction and cover any squares you wish.

When you are ready to continue, enter a starting cell below and click start to begin.

once you have completed your password you will no longer be able to continue moving. When you are done, click the finish button to store your password and then attempt to re enter the passcode. Click finish when you beleive you have entered the correct passcode to continue.
